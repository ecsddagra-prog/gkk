## Component Breakdown

### 1. Header (Fixed)
- Always visible at top
- Quick access to notifications and logout
- Primary CTA: "Book Service"

### 2. Welcome Section
- Personalized greeting
- 3 highlight cards (Wallet, Cashback, Rewards)
- Clickable cards for quick navigation

### 3. Multi-Service Slider â­ (PRIMARY FEATURE)
- Horizontal scrollable
- Auto-shuffle every 4 seconds
- Smart badges (New, Trending, Booked Before)
- Click to select service
- Navigation arrows + dots

### 4. Provider Preview (Conditional)
- Only shows when service is selected
- Dynamic content based on selection
- 4 providers initially, expandable
- Rich provider information
- Click to open booking modal

### 5. Quick Access (Compact)
- 4 quick links in single row
- Shows live data (balance, count)
- Non-intrusive design
- Mobile: 2x2 grid

### 6. Quick Navigation
- 4 large action cards
- Visual icons
- Redundant with Quick Access but more prominent
- Better for first-time users

### 7. Recent Bookings
- Last 10 bookings
- Status indicators
- Quick view details
- Empty state with CTA

### 8. Mobile Sticky Button
- Only on mobile
- Fixed at bottom
- Always accessible
- Primary booking CTA
